About HealthConnect

HealthConnect is a community-focused healthcare platform that helps patients connect with doctors online, get prescriptions instantly, and even order medicines for home delivery. We aim to make healthcare more accessible, especially when patients are away from their hometown or cannot visit a hospital immediately.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare services by providing a digital-first solution. Whether it’s consulting with a doctor, getting a prescription, or placing a medicine order, HealthConnect ensures healthcare is just a click away.

Key Features

  • ✔ Easy doctor consultation online
  • ✔ Upload prescription & order medicines
  • ✔ Multiple delivery addresses for flexibility
  • ✔ Secure and private health data management
